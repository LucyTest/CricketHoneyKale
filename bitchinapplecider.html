Bitchin' Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • 1 part Goldschlager
  • 3 parts Apple juice

Mixing instructions:

Add Apple Juice to Goldschlager and Stir.

Creator/contributor's comments:

Try it warm during the winter months.

Drink Information:
Category: Punch / Party Drink
Alcohol: Alcoholic
Serve in: Punch bowl

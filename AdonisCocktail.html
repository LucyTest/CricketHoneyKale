Adonis Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 1 1/2 oz dry Sherry
  • 1 dash Orange bitters

Mixing instructions:

Stir all ingredients with ice, strain contents into a cocktail glass, and serve.

Drink Information:
Category: Ordinary Drink
Alcohol: Alcoholic
Serve in: Cocktail glass
